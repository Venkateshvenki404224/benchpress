Everything you need to self-host Frappe

No vendor lock-in. No monthly bills. Your infrastructure, your control.

One-Click Deploy
Create a full Frappe bench with your chosen apps. Docker handles the heavy lifting under the hood.
Instant Domains
Traefik auto-routes your site to a public subdomain with automatic SSL certificate provisioning.
WireGuard SSH
Download a VPN config and SSH directly into your container. Encrypted tunnel, no exposed ports.
Multi-App Support
Install ERPNext, HRMS, LMS, Wiki, Insights, Helpdesk — pick exactly what you need per bench.
Real-time Logs
Watch your deployment happen live with streaming terminal output. Know exactly what's happening.
Version Control
Choose Frappe v14 or v15. Pin specific app branches. Reproducible environments every time.

Three steps. That's it.

From zero to a running Frappe site in under 5 minutes.

1
Configure

Pick your Frappe version, select the apps you want, and set your domain name.

2
Deploy

One click. We pull the Docker image, configure Traefik labels, and set up WireGuard.

3
Access

Your site is live on a public domain. Download the WireGuard config and SSH in.
